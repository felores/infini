# Contributor Agreement

Contributor License Agreement, see [CLA.md](https://github.com/basketikun/infinite-canvas/blob/main/CLA.md).

By submitting a Pull Request, patch, documentation, assets, or other contributions to this repository, you agree to this agreement. Maintainers may also ask you to explicitly reply in your PR:

```text
I have read and agree to CLA.md.
```

## Before Contributing, Please Confirm

- You have the right to submit the relevant code, documentation, or assets.
- Third-party content is clearly sourced and licensed compatibly with this project.
- Submissions can be released under AGPL-3.0 and the project's relevant commercial license.
- Do not disclose security vulnerability details through public Issues; follow the [vulnerability reporting](/docs/support/security) process.
